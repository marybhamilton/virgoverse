VIRGO VERSE

Ocean Tell Me

debut album out everywhere now

Virgo Verse is a Singer/Songwriter from Massachusetts living in Los Angeles, California. With a folky pop sound, her debut album "Ocean Tell Me" sings of growing up in a coastal town through the personification of the ocean. These 11 songs document her cross-country move and include themes of introspection, exploration, nostalgia, change, relationships, living presently, and defining home. This alternative record can be listened to while looking out to the sea or wherever you happen to find solace in nature.
